4: Consistency and Standards

The Tower Archive achieves a high degree of both internal and external consistency. The system preserves user expectations and enables navigation through JSTOR’s interface framework through its adoption of conventional visual symbols and consistent design elements.

  • Every page displays the official CUA logo which serves to maintain institutional identity and confirm users stay within the university's digital space.
  • Every page and subcollection contains a search bar which shows the total number of available items while keeping navigation and feedback consistent throughout all sections.
  • The download, save, copy and cite options on individual item pages use familiar standard icons. The symbols follow established digital research standards which make it easier for users who are new to the system.
